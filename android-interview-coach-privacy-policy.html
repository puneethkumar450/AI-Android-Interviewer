01Information we collect

Android Interview Coach is designed to work primarily with local app data. Depending on the features you use, the app may store:

  • Bookmarked questions and progress history
  • App preferences such as theme and dynamic color settings
  • Imported or bundled question packs
  • Interview responses or prompts when you choose AI-assisted features
02How information is used

Your data is used to provide the app experience, including:

  • Restoring practice progress and bookmarked questions
  • Personalizing app behavior and display settings
  • Running mock interview and AI-assisted learning flows you explicitly start
  • Improving release stability, debugging, and operational support when diagnostic tooling is enabled
03Local storage and device data

The app stores most of its working data locally on your device. That includes progress, bookmarks, question packs, and settings required for offline-first use. You can clear the app data through the device settings or use the in-app reset options where available.

04AI and remote services

Some experiences may use remote AI services, such as mock interview analysis or answer feedback. When those features are used, the content you provide may be sent to the configured service provider to generate a response. Only use these features for content you are comfortable sharing for that purpose.

05Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be shared only when required to operate an online feature you requested, comply with legal obligations, protect the service, or investigate abuse or security incidents.

06Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect app data. No storage or transmission system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, so users should avoid submitting highly sensitive personal information through optional AI flows.

07Your choices
  • Use the app offline for local practice where supported
  • Reset progress and remove local data from the app
  • Avoid AI-assisted features if you do not want interview content sent to remote services
  • Uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data subject to Android system behavior
08Children's privacy

Android Interview Coach is intended for interview preparation and professional learning. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children through the app.

09Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. When updates are made, the effective date on this page will be revised to reflect the current version.
